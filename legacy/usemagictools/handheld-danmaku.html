About Handheld Danmaku

Handheld danmaku is a new interactive communication tool that can easily add fun and interactivity to various outdoor activities, performances and carnivals. It features lightweight, portable and easy-to-operate advantages, making every participant part of the event. Handheld danmaku can also express emotions and ideas through pre-written text and emojis, enabling communication and interaction. In the age of social media, the use of handheld danmaku also brings wider social effects, increasing the interactivity and reach of events. Whether for organizers or participants, handheld danmaku is a valuable interactive tool.

FAQ

How to display text in fullscreen?

After clicking start, the phone will automatically enter fullscreen mode. Landscape orientation is recommended.

Can I adjust the scroll speed?

Yes, you can adjust the speed in settings, with multiple speed options available from slow to fast.

Does it support emoji?

Yes, you can input emoji and they will display and scroll normally.

How to exit fullscreen?

Double-tap anywhere on the screen or press the back button to exit.

Does it support portrait mode?

Yes, but landscape mode works better with larger and more visible text.
